River Heights Animal Hospital

Team

Meet the experienced and compassionate team at River Heights Animal Hospital in Saskatoon, dedicated to providing gentle, personalized care for your pets.

At River Heights Animal Hospital, we believe that exceptional veterinary care starts with a team that truly cares. Since 1979, our clinic has been home to a group of experienced, compassionate professionals committed to the health and happiness of your pets.

From our knowledgeable reception staff to our licensed veterinarians with over a decade of hands-on experience, every member of our team brings a deep love for animals and a genuine desire to support pet owners in Saskatoon. We treat each dog and cat as if they were our own—offering gentle handling, clear communication, and personalized care with every visit.

We’re proud of the long-standing relationships we’ve built within the community and invite you to get to know the friendly faces behind your pet’s care.
